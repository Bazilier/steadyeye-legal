Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SteadyEye is a teleprompter app developed by Kirill Vasilyev (sole developer). Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data SteadyEye handles and how.

What stays on your device

Your scripts and text are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. They are never uploaded to any server. Video recordings are saved directly to your Photo Library and never leave your device. Speech recognition for the voice-tracking feature runs entirely on-device using Apple's Speech framework — no audio data is sent to any server.

What is sent externally

When you use the "Optimize for Reading" feature, your script text is sent to the Anthropic Claude API for processing. The text is used solely to format your script for teleprompter use and is not stored by us. Anthropic processes this data under their own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy.

Analytics

SteadyEye uses TelemetryDeck for anonymous, privacy-friendly usage analytics. No personal information, IP addresses, or device identifiers are collected. TelemetryDeck's privacy policy is available at telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

Subscriptions

Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat, which communicates with Apple's StoreKit. RevenueCat receives anonymous transaction data from Apple to manage your subscription status. No personal information is shared. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy. All payments are processed by Apple.

Permissions

SteadyEye requests access to your camera (for video recording), microphone (for audio recording), Photo Library (to save recorded videos), and speech recognition (for voice-activated teleprompter advancement). These permissions are used solely for their stated purposes and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings.

What we do not do

We do not create user accounts or collect emails, names, or any personal information. We do not use advertising or ad-tracking. We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use cookies or web tracking.

Data retention

All data is stored locally on your device. When you delete the app, all local data is removed. There is no server-side data to delete.

GDPR and CCPA

Since SteadyEye does not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no personal data to access, export, or delete. The AI optimization feature processes text transiently and does not retain it.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
